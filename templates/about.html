How It Works

This tool analyzes Terms & Conditions documents using pure Python — no AI, no external APIs, no data leaving your machine.

1
Document Type Detection

A weighted keyword-matching engine scans the text for domain-specific vocabulary (e.g. "premium" + "claim" + "deductible" → Insurance Policy). The type with the most pattern matches wins.

2
Risk Scoring

Over 20 weighted regex patterns scan for aggressive clauses — "binding arbitration", "no refund", "sell personal data", etc. Scores are summed and mapped to Low / Medium / High.

3
Key Point Extraction

Dedicated detectors for 11 categories (Payments, Privacy, Cancellation, Liability, Arbitration, IP, Termination, Renewal, Cookies, Changes, Governing Law) each scan the text for relevant patterns and return a plain-English summary.

4
Red Flag Detection

14 red-flag rules look for particularly concerning clauses and surface them explicitly so nothing hides in the fine print.

5
Before-Signing Checklist

Based on what was detected, a contextual checklist is generated — only items relevant to this specific document appear.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This tool provides a general overview only. It is not legal advice. For important agreements, always consult a qualified legal professional.
